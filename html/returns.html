Returns & Exchanges

We want you to love your SMILEY products. If you're not completely satisfied, we're here to help.

30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

We stand behind our products with a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied, return your purchase for a full refund.

30-Day Return Window

Returns must be initiated within 30 days of delivery. Contact our customer service team to start the return process.

Original Packaging

Items must be returned in their original packaging with all accessories and documentation included.

Proof of Purchase

Please include your order number or receipt with your return. This helps us process your refund quickly.

How to Return an Item

1

Contact Customer Service

Email us at returns@smiley.com or call 1-800-SMILEY-1 to initiate your return.

2

Receive Return Label

We'll email you a prepaid return shipping label and detailed instructions.

3

Package Your Return

Pack the item securely in its original packaging and attach the return label.

4

Ship Your Return

Drop off your package at any authorized shipping location or schedule a pickup.

5

Receive Your Refund

Once we receive and process your return, you'll receive your refund within 3-5 business days.
