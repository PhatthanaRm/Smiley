Our Story

SMILEY was born from a simple idea: oral care doesn't have to be boring! As parents, we struggled to get our kids excited about brushing their teeth. That's when we decided to create something different.

We combined the expertise of dental professionals with the creativity of flavor scientists to develop toothpaste that kids actually want to use. The result? A collection of delicious, effective, and fun oral care products that make everyone smile.

Our Mission

"To transform daily oral care from a chore into a delightful experience, making healthy smiles accessible to everyone through innovative, fun, and effective products."

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about SMILEY products

Are SMILEY products safe for children?

Yes! All our products are dentist-approved and made with natural ingredients. They're safe for the whole family, including children 2 years and older.

Do your products contain fluoride?

Yes, our toothpaste contains the recommended amount of fluoride for effective cavity protection while maintaining our delicious fruit flavors.

How long does shipping take?

We offer free shipping on orders over $25. Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days, and express shipping takes 1-2 business days.

Can I return products if I'm not satisfied?

Absolutely! We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied, we'll refund your purchase, no questions asked.
